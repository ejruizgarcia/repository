English Department AI Generated Resources

Innovative tools powered by AI for modern English teaching

Main Applications

Classroom Tools

Essential classroom utilities including timer, traffic light system, group generator, random student picker, and more interactive tools for effective classroom management.

AI Listening Generator v.02

Generate unlimited listening exercises with corresponding forms. Creates both narrator-style and dialogue-based listening activities using AI technology.

StorySpark

Visual prompt generator for creative writing development. Perfect for speaking lessons too. Generates infinite creative prompts using AI technology.

Reading Challenge

Short reading exercises with audio support. Includes comprehension questions at different levels. Infinite article generation powered by AI.

Rubric Evaluation

Simple rubric-based evaluation system. Generates reports for future reviews and inspections. Streamlined assessment process.
